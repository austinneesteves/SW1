Aspin Aspin came from the word "Asong Pinoy" or "As" + "Pin". Their sizes are medium, usually have a more rough textured fur, and have short coats that can be a variety of colors. These dogs are either brown, white, black. They have spots, or a coat that is brindle.
Golden Retriever The Golden Retriever is a medium sized, muscular and sturdy dogs. They are famous for their lustrous coat of gold that gives the breed its name. With their broad head, friendly and intelligent eyes, short ears, and a straight muzzle, is a kind loving breed .
Shih Tzu Shih Tzu are small dogs weighing 9 to 16 pounds, and height in standing between 8 and 11 inches. Their coats, which comes in many colors and they are as beautiful as a well-groomed dog. They are known for being cute and charming.
Chihuahua Chihuahua is a tiny dog with ha huge personality. They are small weighing not more than 6 pounds. Their eyes are cute and expressive.
